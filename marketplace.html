Marketplace FAQs

How do I get exact details?

Every listing is unique, so we recommend reaching out for a brief scope call. We'll walk you through the account's history, verification status, engagement metrics, and any transfer considerations. This ensures you have complete transparency before making a decision.

What does verification mean here?

Verification signals consistency and authenticity. We check for platform badges, activity patterns, follower quality, and review history. While no marketplace can guarantee future performance, our verification process helps identify accounts with strong credibility signals and minimal red flags.

How fast can a transfer happen?

Transfer timelines vary by platform and account complexity. Simple social media accounts can transfer within 24-48 hours, while more complex digital assets (domains, storefronts, gaming accounts) may take 3-7 days. We'll provide a specific timeline estimate during your scope call based on the asset type and platform requirements.

Can you source something specific?

Absolutely. If you have a specific niche, follower count, or platform in mind, we offer targeted sourcing services. Share your requirements (category, audience demographics, engagement benchmarks) and we'll search our network and partner channels. Keep in mind that highly specific requests may take 1-2 weeks to fulfill depending on availability.

What do you need from me?

To get started, we need four key pieces of information: your target category or niche, your budget range, your timeline (immediate vs. flexible), and your risk tolerance (established accounts vs. growth potential). This helps us match you with the right listings and ensures a smooth transaction from inquiry to transfer.
